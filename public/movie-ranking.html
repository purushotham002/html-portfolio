The Best Movies According to Vijay

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pushpa-The rise

It's an intense action drama about a red sandalwood smuggler's rise to power.

Pagal

It's a quirky romantic comedy about a man searching obsessively for true love.

Geetha Govindam

It's a charming romantic tale of a well-mannered lecturer who falls for a strong-willed woman.
